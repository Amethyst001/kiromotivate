# Kiro Vibe-Coding Prompts

## Daily Motivation Generator
Generate a short, uplifting motivational sentence (max 15 words). 
Focus on:
- Positivity and encouragement
- Actionable mindset
- Universal appeal
- Include an emoji for visual appeal

## Idea Name Generator  
Generate a catchy, memorable name for the given product/idea concept.
Requirements:
- 1-2 words maximum
- Easy to pronounce and remember  
- Professional yet creative
- Avoid generic terms
- Consider the target audience and industry

Context will be provided as: "Generate a name for: [user input]"
